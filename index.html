What is NVP?

The Nepal Volunteer Program was formed in 2019 to create lifelong connections between the UK and Nepal through volunteer placements.

Our volunteers have worked in schools, universities, observatories, engineering firms, hospitals, and physiotherapy clinics. Volunteering is an incredible way to exchange skills and perspectives.
